(**तस्य**, tasya) = whose; being (i.e. ishvaras)  
(**वाचकः**, vācakaḥ) = verbal symbol; word  
(**प्रणवः**, praṇavaḥ) = a symbol that always retains its purity; the OM symbol; the OM mantra
